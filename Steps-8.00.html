All Dax Formulas, Tables and Columns 8






Create Date Table:


1.   Click the ribbon at the top of the screen & click New Table.

2.   Input this Dax Formula ---->     DATE = CALENDAR(DATE(2016,2,23),DATE(2021,1,6))

3. Once done press enter.



CREATE NEW COLUMN TO GET THE DAY OF THE WEEK

 

1.   Right click on one of your Dax measures and click New column

2.   Type this formula below in the bar

3.   Day of Week Name = FORMAT('Date'[Date], "dddd")

4.   Follow the same steps above and type formula below in the bar

5.   Week Day = WEEKDAY('Date'[Date],1)


ALL DAX FORMULAS BELOW:



Tornadoes Per Yr


1.   .Total Tornadoes = COUNT(TornadoData[EVENT_TYPE])

 

2.    Tornadoes Per Yr = AVERAGEX(VALUES('Date'[Date].[Year]), [.Total Tornadoes])

 

 

 

USE FORMULA BELOW TO REMOVE BLANKS IF PRESENT

 

1.   Tornadoes Per Yr Blank = IF([Tornadoes Per Yr],[Tornadoes Per Yr],0)

 

 

 

 YoY



1.   .Total Tornadoes LY = CALCULATE([.Total Tornadoes], DATEADD('Date'[Date],-1,YEAR))

 

2.   .Tor Dif = CALCULATE([.Total Tornadoes] - [.Total Tornadoes LY])

 

3.   .YoY% = DIVIDE([Tor Dif], [.Total Tornadoes LY])     (Make this a percentage)

 

 

 

  Cost Per Tornado

 

1.   .Cost Per Tor = AVERAGE(TornadoData[PROPERTY DAMAGE $])

 

 

 

 


CpT YoY

 

1.   .Property Dmg = CALCULATE(SUM(TornadoData[PROPERTY DAMAGE $]))

 

2.   .YoY Tor Cost Avg = AVERAGEX(VALUES('Date'[Date].[Year]), [Property Dmg])

 

3.   .Tor Ly cost per tor = CALCULATE( [YoY Tor Cost Avg], DATEADD('Date'[Date], -1, YEAR) )

 

4.   .YoY Tor Change % = ([YoY Tor Cost Avg] - [Tor Ly cost per tor])

 

5.   .YoY% CPT =  DIVIDE ([YoY Tor Change %], [Tor Ly cost per tor])    (Make this a percentage)
